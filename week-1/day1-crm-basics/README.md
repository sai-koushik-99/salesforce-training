What is CRM?
-CRM means Customer Relationship Management.
-It is used by companies to manage customer data and sales work in one place.

CRM helps to:
-Store customer details
-Track sales
-Follow up with customers
-Improve communication

What is Salesforce?
-Salesforce is a platform which is used for handling customers,sales,bussiness operation.

Companies use Salesforce to:
-Manage customers
-Track sales
-Store business data
-Automate work
-Why companies use Salesforce
-Easy to manage customer data
-Helps sales teams
-Saves time using automation

Account means a company
Example:
Infosys, Amazon, TCS

Contact
Contact means Person related to the company
Example:
Manager, Customer, Student

Opportunity
Opportunity means Possible deal or business chance
Example:
A customer planning to buy a product

Business Flow
Lead → Contact → Opportunity → Customer

Lead
Person shows interest
Real-Life Mapping (College Admission)
Account = College
Contact = Student
Lead = Student inquiry for admission
Opportunity = Admission process

Playground
Salesforce Playground is a practice environment used for learning and testing Salesforce.
